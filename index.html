Risk control

Designed to reduce hallucinations.

The assistant does not confirm real bookings, live slots, exact prices, refunds, or safety guarantees. When the answer needs current or sensitive data, it escalates to human support.

  • No live booking confirmation without booking data.
  • No invented artisan verification.
  • No unsupported exact price claims.
  • Escalation for no-show, accessibility, refunds, and safety concerns.
